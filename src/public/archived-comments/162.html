  • Jim Mathies on Monday, November 18, 2013 (09:11:02) says:

    I think it just comes down to the current breadth of the Windows tablet computing market. We designed a touch centric tablet browser but the number of devices sold thus far that fit that need is pretty small. I don’t think this is indicative of the success or failure of this market though, it’s just not taking off as fast as we hoped. In time though I bet it will, and we’ll probably end up revisiting our decision. The selection of devices continues to expand and improve, see for example the recently announced Thinkpad 8. Eventually I’d bet sales of these types of devices will rival that of iPad sales.
